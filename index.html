Enneagram Type 1
The Reformer
The Spread of Sacred Standards

For the one who holds the world to a higher standard — and quietly holds herself to an even higher one. This spread invites release of the inner critic and trust in the wisdom of imperfection.

✦ Tap each card to reveal the question ✦

I
The Standard
✦ Touch to reveal
Where am I holding myself to an impossible ideal, and what truth lives beneath that pressure?
II
The Critic
✦ Touch to reveal
What is my inner critic trying to protect me from — and is that protection still serving me?
III
The Reform
✦ Touch to reveal
Where in my life is growth asking me to soften rather than straighten?
IV
The Grace
✦ Touch to reveal
What would I allow myself if I truly believed I was already enough?
✦ Spread Intention

May I release the grip of perfection and remember that goodness lives in the becoming, not only the being done.

Enneagram Type 2
The Helper
The Spread of the Giving Heart

For the one who pours endlessly from her cup, often forgetting to refill it. This spread calls her back to herself — her needs, her wants, her worth beyond what she gives.

✦ Tap each card to reveal the question ✦

I
The Gift
✦ Touch to reveal
What am I giving from love — and what am I giving from fear of not being loved?
II
The Hidden Need
✦ Touch to reveal
What do I most need right now that I haven't allowed myself to ask for?
III
The Boundary
✦ Touch to reveal
Where is Spirit inviting me to receive instead of give?
IV
The Worth
✦ Touch to reveal
What would I know about my own value if I stopped measuring it through others?
✦ Spread Intention

May I turn my loving hands toward myself, trusting that my needs are not a burden but a birthright.

✦ Tap each card to reveal the question ✦

I
The Mask
✦ Touch to reveal

II
The Goal
✦ Touch to reveal
Is what I'm striving toward truly mine — or am I chasing someone else's definition of success?
III
The Pause
✦ Touch to reveal
What does my heart say when the doing stops and the silence begins?
IV
The Real
✦ Touch to reveal
Who am I beneath the achievements — and what does she long for?

III
The Belonging
✦ Touch to reveal

The Expression
